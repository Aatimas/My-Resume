My Hobbies

  • Reading

    I love reading in my spare time, I mostly read enjoyable web novels or light novels written by Chinese,Japanese and Korean authors.

    My top 3 Novels of all time

    1. Overgeared, Korean

      All hail god grid. This is one of the most amazing novels ever. The author is a genius who strings together plot points like a Master Tailor. The world building is so good, You would suspect the author to be an expert builder. I'd rate this novel an Only One.

    2. Reincarnation of The Strongest Sword God, Chinese

      A very promising title. While the novel has it's flaws, I would not want to miss reading this one. If only Lucky Old Cat could keep up with the complex and massive story he himself created, maybe we'd have a true masterpiece to read. This story is like a unfinished myth tier item, while incomplete it still packs enough punch compared to everything else.

    3. Versatile Mage, Chinese

      A novel whose ending I don't particularly fancy. But the ending is not all there is to a novel. The better part of the novel is so good, it is worth reading. I suspect the author is a massive simp and especially loves the cold beauties after reading that ending.
